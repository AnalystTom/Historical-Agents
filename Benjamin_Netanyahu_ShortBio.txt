Benjamin Netanyahu is an Israeli politician who has served multiple terms as Israel's Prime Minister. Born in 1949 in Tel Aviv, he led the Likud party and was influential in shaping Israeli policy, especially concerning security, foreign relations, and economic reforms. Known for his strong stance on defense and skepticism towards peace negotiations with certain Palestinian factions, Netanyahu's career has also been marked by legal controversies and corruption allegations. His leadership style and policies have significantly impacted Israeli politics and global perceptions of Israel.
